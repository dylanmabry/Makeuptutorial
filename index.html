Makeup tutorial

1.First you want to apply your primer and foundation. A good cheap prime is the Maybelline Baby Skin Primer. A good foundation is the Milani foundation.
2.Second you want to apply a concelar to cover your belmishes and any uneven coloring. A good concealar is the Tarte Tape Shape.
3.Next you want to apply a power to set your base. A good powder is the Airspun power.
4.Then you want to move to the eyes. First you can do your eyebrows, i reconmend a eyebrow pencil. Then you want to start your eyeshadow.
5.For your eyeshadow you always want to start with the lightest color and bulid your way up. Always blend.
6.Once your eyeshadow is done you are going to want to add some eyeliner. You can either just have it straight across your eye or make it into a wing. Once this is done you are going to apply eyeliner.
7. Once the eyeliner is done you can start appling mascara and even potentially false eyelashes.
8. Now that your eyes are done you are going to move on to your lips. Start by lining your lips with a color darker than the lip stick.
9. Then apply the lip color.
10. Then fianlly you will put on setting spary.
